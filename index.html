About International Class

HELP University was founded in 1986 to provide affordable quality educational opportunities for Malaysians. It has since developed into a leading institution of higher learning in Malaysia with an international reputation among universities, research organisations, scholars, business, corporate leaders, and governments. HELP University offers a wide and diverse range of programmes covering business, law, management, economics, IT, the social sciences, and the humanities at undergraduate and graduate levels.

ITB Stikom Bali cooperate with Help University in 2005. This program requires relatively the same time as Regular Class which is 4 years and a maximum of 4.5 years, it can even be faster 3.5 years if students have more abilities. This dual degree program specializes in information systems study programs that focus on data analysis and software development. The 70% lecture module was adopted from HELP University, lectures using video conferencing and the full support of the HELP University teaching team. Learning process is done at STIKOM BALI, but there is a special time a few days to visit the HELP campus and Graduation is done at the Kuala Lumpur Malaysia HELP Campus.
